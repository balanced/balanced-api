# Testing


## Credit Status

Use the following bank account numbers to create a credit with a
specific status. Only the following account and routing numbers may be unsed
in the test environment.

### `pending`
`9900000000`<br>
`9900000001`

### `paid`
`9900000002`<br>
`9900000003`

### `failed`
`9900000004`<br>
`9900000005`
